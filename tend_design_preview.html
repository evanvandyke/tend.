The Almanac, Applied

Three signature details, then the system.
Signature 1 · The FAB Shadow
The floating add button casts a tinted forest-green shadow, not a generic black one. box-shadow: 0 4px 14px rgba(45, 82, 53, 0.32). This is one of the most-copied details in productivity design — borrowed from Todoist's tinted red FAB.
Signature 2 · The Bordeaux Period
In the wordmark Tend., the period is always rendered in Bordeaux #7A2E1F. A single deliberate flick of color in an otherwise serene mark.
Signature 3 · Serif Throughout
EB Garamond for display, Lora for body. No sans-serif anywhere in the app. Most productivity apps avoid serifs on phones; Tend. earns readability through size and line-height and gains identity in return.
